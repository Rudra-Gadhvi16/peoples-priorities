About Nirdhar Priority Ledger

Nirdhar is a citizen-submission-to-priority-ledger prototype application designed to bridge public concerns and state budget disbursements under the MPLAD program.

How it works

1. Collection: Citizens log grievances via audio notes, photographs, or text across regional languages.

2. NLP & Classification: Submissions are processed and sorted into developmental themes (Education, Infrastructure, Skilling, Utilities).

3. Context Blending: Hotspots are merged with actual census databases (primary capacity gaps, school distance travel records, etc.).

4. Ledger Calculation: A clear demand ranking formula weights citizen urgency (55%), capacity gap data (30%), and beneficiary targets (15%) to score projects on a 1-100 scale.
